Hello to all who have supported us, what a wild few weeks it has been. We here at Olin Rocketry hope that this finds you safe and healthy. While Rocketry has been on the backburner as the transition away from Olin has occurred, we are now gearing back up for online work and wanted to provide this update to our supporters and followers. It was announced this week that the 2020 Spaceport America Cup would be canceled like many other events this summer, so because of this our plans are now shifting to 2021 as we look to improve our designs and put them through even more rigorous testing. Any competition fees that we have already paid will automatically be shifted to next year as a reservation into the cup so your money will in fact still be going to help propel this team into the Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition. Once again, we thank all of you for your generosity.
